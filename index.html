Common questions

Asked & answered.

What does Ma Pools Construction actually do? add
Pool construction, renovations, marblite and fibreglass work, fish ponds, pump and motor repairs, jacuzzi installs and leak repair, filter sand changes, pool covers, plumbing, heating systems and timers. Outside the pool we also do paving, floor tiles, ceilings, house and office plumbing, and garden services. One contractor for the lot.
Where do you work? add
We're based in Meadowlands, Soweto, and we cover the whole of Gauteng — Soweto, Johannesburg, Roodepoort, Krugersdorp, Diepkloof, Orlando, Pimville, Dobsonville, Lenasia, Florida, Pretoria, Centurion, Midrand and everywhere in between. If you're not sure whether we cover your area, send a quick WhatsApp and we'll confirm.
How fast can you give me a quote? add
Within one business day, often the same day. WhatsApp a photo of your pool, paving area or whatever needs work, with a brief description, and Mkhacani comes back with a price. For bigger jobs (new builds, full renovations) we may book an on-site visit before quoting — also free, also fast.
Do I have to pay for a quote? add
No. Quotes are free and there's no obligation. If you don't like the number, walk away — no awkward follow-ups.
Who do I actually deal with? add
Mkhacani Amos Chauke — the founder and operating director. He answers WhatsApps and phone calls personally. No call centres, no middlemen, no chasing the office to find out what's happening on your job.
Can I just get the pool serviced regularly? add
Yes. We run weekly, fortnightly and monthly service plans — water testing, chemical balancing, filter checks, the works. Tell us your pool size and how often you want a visit and we'll quote a flat monthly amount.
Do you guarantee your work? add
Yes. Workmanship is guaranteed and any equipment we install is covered by the supplier's warranty. If something we did goes wrong, you tell us and we make it right. Full terms are on our Terms page.
Why "Pool" company doing paving and plumbing? add
Because pools rarely live alone. Most of our customers also need their deck repaved, their tile work redone, their plumbing rerouted, or their garden tidied around the new pool. Doing it ourselves means one accountable contractor instead of three different guys you have to chase. Pool is what we lead with — but we handle the whole job.
