Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 April 2026

Ma Pools Construction ("Ma Pools Construction","we","us", or"our") respects the privacy of every person who interacts with our website, services, and team. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act, No. 4 of 2013 (POPIA) of the Republic of South Africa.

By submitting an enquiry, booking a consultation, or otherwise providing your personal information to Ma Pools Construction, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that is reasonably required to provide our services. The categories of information we may collect include:

  • Identity and contact details, full name, email address, telephone number.
  • Estate details, physical address of the property at which services are to be performed, pool type, equipment information, and access instructions.
  • Service correspondence, the contents of any messages, requests, or feedback you send us via the website, email, WhatsApp, or telephone.
  • Technical information, IP address, device type, browser, and pages visited (collected automatically via standard web logs and any analytics tools we deploy).
  • Payment details, handled by our third-party payment providers; Ma Pools Construction does not store full card numbers on its own systems.

2. How We Use Your Information

We process your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To respond to enquiries, provide quotations, and schedule consultations.
  • To deliver, manage, and invoice for the pool care services you have engaged us to perform.
  • To communicate operational matters, service confirmations, technician arrival times, equipment recommendations, emergency notifications.
  • To improve our website, our service offerings, and the client experience.
  • To comply with our legal, regulatory, tax, and accounting obligations in South Africa.

3. Lawful Basis for Processing

Under POPIA, we rely on the following grounds to process your personal information:

  • Consent, when you submit a form, request a quotation, or subscribe to communications.
  • Contractual necessity, to perform the pool care services we have agreed to provide.
  • Legitimate interest, to operate, secure, and improve our business in a manner that does not unreasonably impact your rights.
  • Legal obligation, to comply with tax, accounting, and other applicable South African laws.

4. Sharing of Information

Ma Pools Construction does not sell your personal information. We share information only with:

  • Our employees and field technicians, on a need-to-know basis, in order to deliver services.
  • Trusted third-party operators acting on our behalf, including hosting providers, email providers, payment processors, and accounting services. These operators are contractually bound to keep your information confidential and to process it only on our instructions.
  • Regulatory authorities, courts, or law-enforcement agencies, where required by South African law.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law:

  • Enquiries that do not progress to a service engagement, retained for up to 12 months, then deleted.
  • Active client records, retained for the duration of the service relationship.
  • Records required for tax, accounting, or statutory purposes, retained for the minimum period required by South African law (typically five years).

6. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal information from loss, unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure. These measures include encrypted transmission (HTTPS), access controls, secure third-party providers, and ongoing staff training. While we work hard to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is entirely secure.

7. Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under POPIA, you have the following rights in relation to your personal information:

  • The right to be notified that your personal information is being collected.
  • The right to request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • The right to request correction or deletion of inaccurate, irrelevant, excessive, outdated, or unlawfully obtained information.
  • The right to object, on reasonable grounds, to the processing of your personal information.
  • The right to withdraw your consent at any time, where processing is based on consent.
  • The right to lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator (South Africa).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@mapoolsconstruction.co.za. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe and in any event within the period required by law.

8. Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses essential cookies to function and may use privacy-respecting analytics (such as Plausible) to understand aggregate traffic patterns. We do not use intrusive cross-site tracking technologies. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.

9. Cross-Border Transfers

Some of our service providers (for example, hosting and email infrastructure) may process data outside South Africa. Where this occurs, we ensure that the recipient is subject to laws or contractual safeguards that provide an adequate level of protection in line with POPIA.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in law or in our practices. The"Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised. We encourage you to review it periodically.

11. Contact Us

For any questions about this policy, requests to exercise your rights, or concerns about how Ma Pools Construction handles your personal information, please contact our Information Officer:

  • Email: privacy@mapoolsconstruction.co.za
