# Pool Care Company Website - Gauteng, South Africa

## Project Overview
Design a high-end website for a premium pool maintenance and care company based in Gauteng. The target audience is the higher class of South Africa, demanding excellence, reliability, and professionalism.

## Core Screens
1. **Landing Page:** Sophisticated overview, premium imagery, Gauteng focus, clear CTA.
2. **Services Overview:** Detailed maintenance, repair, and specialized offerings.
3. **Customer Testimonials:** Dedicated section/page showcasing satisfied high-end clients.
4. **Booking/Inquiry Form:** Streamlined, elegant lead capture form.
5. **Gallery/Portfolio:** High-quality visuals of pristine pools under care.
6. **About Us/Contact:** Expertise highlights and clear contact info.

## Visual Direction
- **Style:** Modern, clean, elegant, professional.
- **Colors:** Deep blues, whites, subtle metallic accents (gold/silver).
- **Typography:** Refined serif headings, clean sans-serif body.
- **Imagery:** High-resolution photos of luxury pools and professional service.
